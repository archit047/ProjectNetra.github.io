Project Netra

Smart systems for precision agriculture

Namaste!

This project was initially developed during the course of 2 undergraduate research internships at an NGO NEFORD (Nand Educational Foundation for Rural Development) under advisory of it's Executive Director Dr. R.K.Singh and with the voluntary supervision of Dr. Chhedi Lal Verma at Central Soil Salinity Research Institute (Regional Research Station Lucknow), India. It was developed with the aim of analyzing the field state and identifying unique patterns pertaining to the logged data. With the focus on developing cheap, automated, scalable systems using Digital Signal Processing, Machine Learning and Embedded Systems, leveraging the domain knowledge.
